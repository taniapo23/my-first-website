Oils

An oil is the result of macerating vegetables or fruit, whose properties will be yielded to the oil. Once spead on the skin, the oil will be extrememly beneficial.

Woods fruits oil

Ingredient Quantity Properties
Rosehip Berry 1 piece Rich in vitamin C, anti-aging, brightening, smoothing, gives tone.
Blueberries 1 tbsp Rich in potassium, phosphorus, calcium, vitamin C,A and E, antioxidant, anti-aging, effective on couperose.
Blackberries 1tbsp Rich in iron and calcium, antioxidant.
Strawberries 1 tbsp Revitalizing, brightening, astringent, purifying.
Carrier Oil (sweet almond, Rapeseed, Olive Oil...) Enough to cover all the ingredients It helps in the even distribution of the product.

    Preparation

  • Put all the ingredients in a container and leave them infused for 40 days in a dry, fresh and dark place, shaking it every 2 days;
  • After 40 days, filter it with a cotton sheet, by squeezing the fruits very well;
  • Wash and sterilise a glass jar and pour the oil in it.
