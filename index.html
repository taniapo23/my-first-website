What does "NATURAL" mean?

When it comes to the term “natural,” there are no strict definitions, and as a consumer, a lot depends on your own personal choice.

When considering if something is natural you can look at two factors:

  • What it is derived from. Is it from a natural source? For example, from plants, animals, minerals, marine sources, or is it derived from petrochemicals, and therefore considered synthetic?
  • How it is processed or modified. Naturally derived ingredients can be processed physically (they are only subjected to physical processes and the molecular composition/structure stays the same) or chemically (they are subjected to at least one chemical process and the molecular composition/structure changes) or both.
